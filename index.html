The Wedding

On the 13th of May, 2023, Francisco Power and Angélica Núñez will tie the knot. If you're here, it's probably because you got an invite to travel to Idanha-a-Nova, Portugal, and celebrate the creation of this new family. Here, you'll find some precious information and, hopefully, the answers to all the questions that are on your mind.

Where do I stay?

From campsite to hotel,here are many options. We suggest the Hotel Estrela de Idanha, which, besides being the lunch location and very close to the Power Family House, has a lot of fun stuff to offer, such as a swimming pool, a tennis court and more.

You can book a place there by writing an email direcly to reservas@estreladaidanha.pt or you can just talk to us and we'll take care of it for you.

The Wedding church

The wedding will be in a beautiful little church, Igreja Matriz de São Salvador, in an amazing place, Monsanto. It's a beautiful 30 minute drive form the village of Idanha-a-Nova.

Mass will be celebrated in Portuguese, with some readings and songs in English and Spanish. Don't worry, there will be a cute little booklet with everything translated into your language.

The Power Family House

The Power family lives in a mansion (well, not really, but it's a big old house) in the heart of Idanha-a-Nova.

This will be the location of the party, after lunch. There will be cake, magic, dancing, games, and tons of fun! You're also invited to share your talents with us. Surprise us!

Dresscode

KISS: Keep It Simple, Silly.

There is no "specific" dresscode, we don't want you to spend unnecessary money. Something elegant and appropriate for mass will do great.
