Francisco & Angélica

The Wedding

On the 13th of May, 2023, Francisco Power and Angélica Núñez will tie the knot. If you're here, it's probably because you got an invite to travel to Idanha-a-Nova, Portugal, and celebrate the creation of this new family. Here, you'll find some precious information and, hopefully, the answers to all the questions that are on your mind.

Where do I stay?

From campsite to hotel,here are many options. We suggest the Hotel Estrela de Idanha. You can book a place there by writing an email direcly to reservas@estreladaidanha.pt

  • Hotel Estrela-de-Idanha

"Food, glorious food!"

Do you or someone in your family have some food restriction, like glúten or lactose intolerance? Just send us an email detailing the situation, we'll take care of it.

  • Wedding menu

The Wedding church

The wedding will be in a beautiful little church, Igreja Matriz de S. Salvador, in an amazing place, Monsanto. Its a beautiful 30 minute drive form the village of Idanha-a-Nova.

The Power Family House

The Power family lives in a mansion (well, not really, but it's a big old house) in the heart of Idanha-a-Nova. This will be the location of the party, after lunch.
